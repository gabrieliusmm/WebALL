Blue

Blue is the color of the sky and sea. It is often associated with depth and stabillity. It symbolizes trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith, truth and heaven.

Green

Green is the color of natue. It symbolizes growth, harmony, freshness and fertillity. Green has strong emotional correspondence with safety

Red

Red is the color of fire and blood, so it is associated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire and love.
